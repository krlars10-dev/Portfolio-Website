Professional Web Brand

TrueBit Forge North

TrueBit Forge North is my business brand focused on premium web design, custom front-end builds, digital presentation, and polished online experiences for businesses that want to look more credible and modern.

The brand is built around intentional design, clear messaging, responsive development, and business growth through a stronger digital presence.

Golf Product Platform

GoodFlightGolf

GoodFlightGolf is a golf-focused web platform centered on on-course games, club distance logging, handicap tools, and interactive features designed to make the experience more useful and more fun for players.

The project blends product thinking, branding, custom calculators, and user-first functionality into a focused niche experience for golfers.
